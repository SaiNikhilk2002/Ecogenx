Who We Are Get Started

Welcome to EcogenX Innovations

We are a Hyderabad-based waste management and sustainability startup making waste disposal simple, rewarding, and responsible. Through doorstep collections, smart recycling bins, and awareness programs, we help households, schools, and businesses turn waste into resources — building cleaner communities and a greener India.

Together, We Can Make a Difference

Keep Our Streets Clean

By providing visible waste disposal points — from homes to public areas like tea stalls, pan shops, schools, and offices.

Empower Local Communities

By creating jobs for women, students, and local youth through our waste collection and awareness programs.

Build Responsible Habits

By making waste disposal easy, rewarding, and part of daily life for every Indian.

Turn Waste into Resources

By collecting, recycling, and converting waste into new products or clean energy — reducing pollution and landfill burden.
