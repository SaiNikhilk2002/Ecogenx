Who We Are Get Started

Welcome to EcogenX Innovations

We are a Hyderabad-based tech-driven waste management startup blending AI, sustainability, and community empowerment. Join our journey to build zero-waste cities through smart bins, waste-to-energy tech, and reward systems.

How Our System Works

1

Smart Waste Collection

Our AI-powered smart bins automatically sort and compact waste while monitoring fill levels in real-time. When full, they alert our collection teams for timely pickup.

2

Automated Sorting

At our processing centers, advanced robotics and computer vision systems separate materials with 95% accuracy, maximizing recyclable recovery.

3

Waste-to-Energy Conversion

Non-recyclable waste is converted to clean energy through our proprietary gasification process, powering local communities.

4

Rewards & Analytics

Users earn EcogenX tokens for proper waste disposal, redeemable for benefits. Municipalities access real-time waste analytics through our dashboard.
