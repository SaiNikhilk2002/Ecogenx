About EcogenX Innovations

We're not just a company. We're a movement leading India into a sustainable, waste-free, and tech-powered future.

Our Story

Born in the heart of Greater Hyderabad, EcogenX Innovations was founded with a mission to revolutionize how India handles waste. We recognized early that the traditional systems were broken — overburdened, inefficient, and harming our environment.

With a powerful mix of smart infrastructure, community collaboration, and incentives, we've turned recycling into a lifestyle and non-recyclables into clean energy.

Our Vision

  • Waste is a resource, not garbage
  • Every home contributes to a greener India
  • Recycling is effortless & everyday
  • Non-recyclables fuel clean energy
  • Future generations live waste-resilient lives

Our Mission

  • Make recycling simple and habitual
  • Smart, doorstep waste collection systems
  • Inspire action via tech and rewards
  • Build a waste-free circular economy
  • Create social and economic opportunities
  • Convert waste into clean energy

Our Core Innovations

Smart Infrastructure

AI-powered reverse vending, shredding bins, and real-time waste tracking — making recycling tech-savvy and transparent.

Doorstep Convenience

Biodegradable bags, weekly collections, and prompt services for homes, offices, and communities.

Incentive-Driven

Cashbacks, discounts, and more — sustainability is now rewarding for everyone.

Waste-to-Energy

Non-recyclable waste fuels clean energy, supporting off-grid areas and cutting fossil fuel use.

Community Empowerment

Creating jobs for women, spreading awareness, and encouraging grassroots action for lasting change.

Scalable Impact

From Hyderabad to all of India — a growth model built for large-scale, measurable impact.
