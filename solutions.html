Phase 1

Weekly Waste Collection

Overview: EcogenX's Weekly Waste Collection initiative uses biodegradable bags to help residents separate and dispose of recyclable waste. Weekly doorstep pickups encourage recycling at the community level.

Biodegradable Bags: Delivered to homes for easy sorting.
Doorstep Pickups: Carried out by local women weekly.
Sorting Facilities: Waste is processed in EcogenX units.
Incentive Program: Rewards for consistent recyclers.
Phase 2

Cigarette Waste Collection Boxes

Overview:
Tamper-resistant bins placed at cafes, tech parks, colleges, transit hubs, and airports for safe and effective cigarette waste disposal.

Promotes Awareness

Educates about the harmful effects of cigarette waste on the environment.

Reduces Pollution

Decreases microplastic and toxin pollution caused by improper disposal.

Protects Wildlife

Keeps waste from landfills and reduces risks to animals and ecosystems.

Process:
1. Component Separation

Cigarette butts are dismantled into filter, paper, and tobacco.

2. Recycling & Composting

Filters are recycled, paper reused, and tobacco composted safely.

3. Sent to Recycling Centers

Sorted components are delivered to certified recycling facilities.

Phase 3

Segregated Dustbins for Corporates & Schools

Why It Works:
Strategically shaped and color-coded bins encourage individuals — especially students — to dispose of waste correctly. This initiative builds awareness and cultivates eco-friendly habits from a young age.

Easy Awareness

Shaped bins help children and students quickly identify the correct bin for each waste type.

Common Usage

Perfect for schools and malls — paper waste, exam sheets, bottles, cans, and more.

Early Recycling Habits

Fosters a lifelong habit of proper recycling starting from childhood and youth.

Phase 4

E-Waste Collection Bins

Overview:
Smart, transparent bins placed in offices and tech environments to collect batteries, cables, earbuds, and small electronics. These bins simplify safe disposal and help organizations comply with green regulations.

Safe Hazard Disposal

Prevents environmental damage by enabling safe disposal of electronic waste and toxic materials.

Carbon-Saving Metrics

Helps track and report carbon savings, boosting sustainability reporting for companies and schools.

Promotes E-Waste Awareness

Supports eco-education and spreads awareness about proper e-waste handling and recycling.

Phase 5

Transparent Plastic Shredding Bins

Overview:
Interactive, transparent bins that shred plastic waste in real-time, offering a visual and rewarding recycling experience for users.

Use Cases:
Deployed at festivals, metro stations, shopping malls, and public events to drive mass participation and visibility.

Why It Works:
Visually Engaging

Real-time shredding captures user attention and encourages active participation.

Reduces Contamination

Shredding on-site prevents mixing with other waste types, preserving recyclability.

Ready-to-Process Output

Shredded material is directly ready for recycling plants, cutting down processing time.

Phase 6

Smart Reverse Vending Machines (RVMs)

Function:
These smart machines accept plastic bottles, wrappers, and containers, sort them by type and weight, and issue digital reward points that can be redeemed for cashback, travel credits, or shopping discounts.

Deployed At:
  • Supermarkets & Hypermarkets (e.g., D-Mart, Reliance Fresh)
  • Malls & Shopping Complexes
  • Metro & Railway Stations
  • Universities, Public Parks, Corporate Offices
How It Works:
Accepts Plastic Waste

Users deposit used plastic bottles, wrappers, and containers into the machine.

Weighs & Sorts Waste

Automatically detects material type, measures quantity, and sorts appropriately.

Issues Reward Points

Incentivizes recycling with points redeemable for transport, shopping, or cashback.

Phase 7

App-Based Waste Collection

Overview:
A smart plastic collection system designed for communities, households, corporates, and retail chains through the EcogenX mobile app.

Community & Apartment Based Collection:
Dedicated Collection Bags

Residents receive plastic bags for collection — no hassle of sorting themselves.

Weekly Scheduled Pickups

Pickup happens weekly with optimized routes to ensure timely collection.

Weighing & Rewards

Plastic is weighed, and users receive reward points directly on the app.

On-Demand Pickup via App:
1. Store Plastic Waste

Users collect wrappers, oil bottles, chairs, and more in storage.

2. Schedule Pickup

Pickup requests can be made anytime using the EcogenX app.

3. Instant Rewards

Waste is recycled or converted into energy, and users are rewarded instantly.

Why It Works:
Convenience

Home collection removes the burden of dropping off recyclables at centers.

Scalability

Perfectly expandable to corporate offices, restaurants, and retail chains.

Efficiency

AI-based routing boosts logistics, reduces cost, and increases pickup speed.

Incentives for Users:
  • Grocery discounts at D-Mart & Big Bazaar
  • Metro and bus fare reductions
  • Amazon/Flipkart shopping coupons
  • Paytm, PhonePe, Google Pay cashback
  • Discounts on movie tickets and restaurant bills
Phase 8

Circular Waste-to-Energy Conversion

Recycling & Energy Conversion:
Plastic waste is efficiently separated into recyclable and non-recyclable categories. Recyclables are transformed into new products such as packaging and textile fibers, while non-recyclables are converted into usable energy — ensuring nothing ends up in a landfill.

1. Waste Sorting

Plastic is separated into recyclable and non-recyclable types at our facilities.

2. Material Recovery

Recyclable plastics are processed into packaging, textiles, or manufacturing materials.

3. Energy Generation

Non-recyclables are converted into clean energy instead of being landfilled.

Why It Works:
Circular Economy

Every piece of plastic is reused, recycled, or repurposed — nothing goes to waste.

Clean Energy

Provides renewable energy for off-grid communities and backup energy grids.

Fossil Fuel Reduction

Helps reduce reliance on non-renewable resources like coal and oil.
